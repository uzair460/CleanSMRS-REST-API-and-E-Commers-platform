About CleanSMRs

SMRs offer a promising solution for providing reliable, clean, and carbon-free energy to data centers.
Unlike renewable energy sources like wind or solar, which are intermittent, SMRs can deliver constant power 24/7,
making them particularly suitable for the high and continuous energy demands of data centers.
